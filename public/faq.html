Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent a vehicle?
Renting a vehicle is simple! Just select your preferred vehicle, choose the dates, and complete the booking process. You can pay online or in person.
What documents do I need?
You will need a valid driver's license and a form of ID. For some vehicles, additional documents may be required.
Can I cancel my booking?
Yes, you can cancel your booking up to 24 hours before the start date without any charges. After that, cancellation fees may apply.
Are there any age restrictions to rent a vehicle?
Yes, you must be at least 21 years old to rent a vehicle. Drivers under 25 may incur a young driver surcharge.
What happens if I return the vehicle late?
If you return the vehicle late, additional fees may apply. It’s best to inform us in advance if you anticipate a delay.
Do you offer roadside assistance?
Yes, we provide 24/7 roadside assistance for all rental vehicles at no additional charge.
